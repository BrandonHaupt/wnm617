Bee Hive

A beehive is an enclosed, man-made structure in which some honey bee species of the subgenus apis live and raise their young. The bees use the cells to store food (honey and pollen) and to house the brood (egg, larvae, pupae). Can be used to gather honey. lots of ouch if prevoked.
